    About This Tool

    How we calculate your lifetime

    Life expectancy figures are sourced from the WHO Global Health Observatory and World Bank open data, updated annually. Your remaining life is calculated as: (life expectancy − current age) × 365.25 days. All calculations happen entirely in your browser — no data leaves your device.

    The lifetime totals are projections based on your current daily habits remaining constant. Life changes — use the "What If?" tab to explore how small adjustments compound over years.
