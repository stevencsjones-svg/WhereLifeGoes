Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 April 2026

Where Life Goes ("we", "us", "our") operates the website wherelifegoes.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. The short version: we collect very little, we don't sell anything, and your time audit data never leaves your browser.

Information we collect

Information you provide. The time allocation calculator on our homepage collects the values you enter into the sliders — sleep hours, work hours, and so on. This data is processed entirely within your browser. None of it is transmitted to our servers. We have no database of user inputs.

Locally stored data. If you use the calculator and return to the site, your previous inputs may be stored in your browser's local storage to restore your last session. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Analytics. We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how people use the site — page views, referral sources, and general geographic regions. This data is anonymous and aggregated. We do not use cookies for analytics purposes.

Advertising. We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

How we use information

  • To operate and improve the calculator and website
  • To understand how visitors find and use the site
  • To display contextually relevant advertising
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or rent any personal information to third parties.

Cookies

The calculator itself does not use cookies. If Google AdSense is active on the site, it may set cookies for advertising purposes. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or opt out of interest-based advertising through the Digital Advertising Alliance.

Your rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights under GDPR (European Union), POPIA (South Africa), CCPA (California), or similar legislation. Since we do not collect personal data server-side, most of these rights are satisfied by design. If you have questions or requests, contact us at the address below.

Third-party links

Our site contains affiliate links and links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Children

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will note the date of the most recent update at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@wherelifegoes.com
